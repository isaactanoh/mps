Our Story

Founded with a passion for creating clean, healthy environments, MPS Cleaning Services has been serving residential and commercial clients for over a decade. What started as a small local business has grown into a trusted name in professional cleaning services.

Our journey began when our founder recognized the need for reliable, thorough cleaning services that customers could count on. With a commitment to excellence and attention to detail, we've built a reputation for quality that keeps our clients coming back.

Today, we continue to uphold the same values that guided us from the beginning: integrity, professionalism, and a genuine care for our clients' satisfaction.
