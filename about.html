About Becks Cleaning Service

Your trusted partner for all cleaning needs

Our Story

Becks Cleaning Service was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services that make a real difference in people's lives. What started as a one-woman operation has grown into a trusted team of cleaning professionals serving homes and businesses throughout the area.

Miss. Rebecca, our founder, began her cleaning career while working part-time to support her education. She quickly realized her passion for transforming spaces and creating clean, healthy environments for families and businesses. Her attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction became the foundation of our company.

Today, we're proud to have expanded our team while maintaining the personal touch and high standards that set us apart from the beginning.
